Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use Pathway Pro if I'm not a student at UNCC?

No, Pathway Pro is currently only accessible to students affiliated with UNCC.

Is Pathway Pro free to use, or are there any subscription fees?

Pathway Pro is completely free to use! Enjoy access to all features without any subscription fees.

Can I customize my checklist based on my specific career goals and interests?

Absolutely! Pathway Pro allows you to personalize your checklist according to your unique career aspirations and interests.

Can I track my progress and update my checklist as I go?

Yes, Pathway Pro provides tools to track your progress and update your checklist as you make advancements in your career journey.

How frequently is Pathway Pro updated with new features or content?

The Pathway Pro platform will continuously be refreshed with new features, improvements and content. We want Pathway Pro to evolve and keep the platform current to provide the best available career guidance possible.

Can I access Pathway Pro on my mobile device?

No, Pathway Pro is currently optimized for desktop use. While we're working hard to make it accessible on mobile devices, it's not available just yet.

How can I contact customer support if I encounter any issues or have further questions?

If you experience any difficulties or have any questions, you can contact our customer support team via email, phone or through our online support portal to get assistance.
